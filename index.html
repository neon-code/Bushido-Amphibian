The Game

Why play it?

Defeat enemies as Frog Samurai with your sword, stick to walls and ceilings, and unlock new abilities as you fight!
Discover various environments with unique scenery and take on the natural enemies of the frog.
Use your tongue as a hook to fly around the world, and use your innate stickiness to stick to walls!
The combination of setting, music, fluid and fast-paced gameplay pack a punch.

Features

Setting in ancient Japan

Frog tongue dynamics

Stick to walls

Fast gameplay
